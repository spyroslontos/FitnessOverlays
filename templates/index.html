How It Works

Turn any Strava activity into a custom overlay for Instagram Stories in a few simple steps.

1. Connect Strava

Log in with Strava so we can pull your runs, rides, and workouts for your overlay.

2. Pick an Activity

Choose the Strava activity you want to turn into an Instagram Story overlay or stats sticker style graphic.

3. Design Your Overlay

Select stats, colors, fonts, and layout to match your brand or IG aesthetic. Create a clean, transparent Strava overlay in your own style.

4. Export & Share

Copy or download the transparent PNG and add it as a sticker in Instagram Stories, Reels, or TikTok, just like a Strava stats sticker but fully customized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitnessOverlays

FitnessOverlays is a tool that creates customizable, Instagram-ready overlays for your Strava activities. It allows you to showcase your fitness achievements with transparent backgrounds, perfect for social media sharing.
Simply connect your Strava account, select an activity, and customize your overlay with various templates, colors, and stats. Once you're happy with the design, you can download it as a transparent PNG ready to share on Instagram or other platforms.
We ask for read-only access to your Strava activities and some info from your profile to make your experience better. We don't change anything on your account.
  1. Connect your Strava account in FitnessOverlays and select the activity you want to share.
  2. Customize your Strava overlay (stats, colors, layout) and copy or download the transparent PNG.
  3. Open Instagram and create a new Story with your background photo or video.
  4. Add your FitnessOverlays image from your camera roll as a sticker and position it like a Strava stats sticker over your background.

This gives you full control over how your Strava activity appears on Instagram Stories while avoiding the default orange Strava share image.

To completely disconnect FitnessOverlays from your Strava account, you need to revoke access in your Strava settings:
  1. Go to your Strava account settings
  2. Navigate to "My Apps"
  3. Find FitnessOverlays in the list
  4. Click "Revoke Access"

Note: Simply logging out of FitnessOverlays only ends your session but doesn't revoke the app's access to your Strava data.
